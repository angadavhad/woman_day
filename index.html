Sports Icon

Breaking records, winning hearts

Olympic Medalist

P. V. Sindhu

P. V. Sindhu is one of India's most successful badminton players. She won a silver medal at the 2016 Olympic Games and a bronze medal at the 2020 Olympics. In 2019 she became the first Indian to win the BWF World Championship gold medal.

🏅2 Olympic Medals
🏆5 World Medals
🥇1 World Champion

Business Leader

Leading global corporations with vision

Former CEO, PepsiCo

Indra Nooyi

Indra Nooyi served as the CEO of PepsiCo from 2006 to 2018. Under her leadership the company grew into one of the world's most successful food and beverage corporations. She is known for her leadership and innovative global business strategies.

📈12 Years as CEO
💰$63B Revenue
🏆100+ Awards

Political Icon

Shaping the destiny of a nation

First Female PM of India

Indira Gandhi

Indira Gandhi was the first female Prime Minister of India. She served the country for over 15 years and played an important role in shaping India's political history. Her leadership and courage made her one of the most influential political leaders in the world.

🇮🇳15 Years in Office
🌍1st Female PM
⚡1971 War Hero
